The Best Movies According to Melo

My top 3 movies of all-time

Waterboy

This is an Adam Sandler! Sandler plays a 33 year old waterboy who turns into an All American star linebacker.

Varsity Blues

A 2nd string quarterback gets an unexpected shot to start and becomes a star but realizes that grass isn't as greener on the other side.

Troy

The fascination of the warriors tale from ancient Greece with amazing acting.
